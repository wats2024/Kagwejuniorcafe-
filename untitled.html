About Us

Kagwe Junior Café is located in Kilgoris, serving delicious Kenyan traditional dishes. We specialize in outside catering and are open Monday to Friday, primarily catering to government office workers nearby.

Our Menu

We serve a variety of dishes, including:

  • Ugali and Sukuma Wiki
  • Nyama Choma (Grilled Meat)
  • Chapati and Beans
  • Traditional Stews
